# Recording the demo GIF

The demo is the most important asset — a <10s clip of scattered windows snapping into a grid sells TermGrid better than any paragraph.

## Suggested shot

1. Open ~8 terminal windows and scatter/overlap them messily across your screen(s).
2. Start one or two as Claude Code sessions and kick off a task so they're **actively working** (Braille spinner in the title).
3. Start the recording.
4. Press the arrange hotkey (⌘⌥⌃G). Windows snap into the grid; the busy sessions land top-left; overflow spills to the second monitor.
5. Stop the recording.

## Tools

- **[Kap](https://getkap.co/)** (free, open source) — record a region, export GIF or MP4.
- Or macOS built-in: `⇧⌘5` → record selection → then convert the `.mov` to GIF (e.g. with `ffmpeg` or [Gifski](https://gif.ski/)).

## Tips

- Keep it short and loopable.
- Record at 2x (Retina) then scale down — crisper than recording small.
- Drop the result at `demo/termgrid.gif` and update the image link at the top of the README.
